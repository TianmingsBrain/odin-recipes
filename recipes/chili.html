Slowcooker Vegan Chili Recipe

Vegan Chili

This chili is packed with plant-based protein. Every spoonful has black beans and kidney beans, and of course, quinoa. All are fantastic vegan sources of protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 small red onion roughly chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves minced
  • 2 bell peppers diced
  • 2 15oz. cans black beans drained and rinsed
  • 2 15oz. cans kidney beans drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup uncooked quinoa rinsed
  • 2 cups frozen corn
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp hot smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
    • ]
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 28 oz. can crushed tomatoes
  • 6 oz. tomato paste
  • 2 3/4 cups vegetable broth

Instructions

  1. Add the onion, corn, beans, quinoa, garlic, cilantro stems, spices, salt, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and vegetable broth to a slow cooker. Mix well, until everything is evenly distributed.
  2. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 4 to 5 hours, or on high for 3 hours.
  3. Stir well and add additional salt to taste, if necessary. Serve warm; top with the remaining cilantro leaves and any additional toppings, as desired. Leftovers will keep in the fridge for up to 5 days, or in the freezer for up to 2 months.
